About

Hey you made it! Hi, my name is Joe, Game Programmer and Designer. Currently I'm working on a new entry in the Witcher Universe at The Molasses Flood, a CD Projekt Studio. Prior to that, I worked at Invisible Walls, working as a Backend and UI Programmer on First Class Trouble, available on Steam, Epic, and PlayStation Platforms.

While attending school for my Bachelor's Degree in Game Development and Simulation Programming, I knabbed an exciting internship working at Attollo LLC, a government affiliated company with a focus on the RQ-21 Umanned Aircraft Simulators for Operator Training, built mostly in WPF and built with an MVVM framework. Here, I was able to build a pipeline to importing models and assets for Training Vignettes (or Scenarios). I also had the pleasure of getting a contract to build an AR app for Dewetron, (the a-fore-mentioned mobile app), Dewetron AR... Available on Apple Store and as an .APK for Android.

In another life, I also worked as a Graphic Designer and Video Editor for five years, all while practicing in Unreal Engine's blueprints and first learning C++ using Unreal's API.

Check out the projects below for a fuller body of work. . If you'd like to contact me, please feel free via email or preferrably, my LinkedIn. I'll try to respond by end of day!
